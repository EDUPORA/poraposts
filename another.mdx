Remita is a payment solution that helps individuals and businesses make and receive payments, pay bills, and manage their finances across multiple banks on a single platform.

Learn how to make payments on Remita in this essential article for citizens on campus.

No account opening is required. The first step, log on to Remita's official website: www.remita.net, locate the Bills and Payments card and select.
