***Remita*** is a ***payment*** solution that helps individuals and businesses ***make*** and receive ***payments***, ***pay*** bills, and manage their finances across multiple banks on a single platform.

Learn how to make payments on Remita in this essential article for citizens on campus.

No account opening is required. The first step, log on to Remita's official website: [www.remita.net](https://www.remita.net), locate the **Bills and Payments** card and select.

After entering the RRR number you want to pay for, click the '**continue'** button.

The Remita payment confirmation page loads; here you confirm that the pre-filled information is accurate with what you expect them to be.

Then, scroll to the page's bottom and click the '**submit'** button.

A pop-up should overlay the existing view on the page. In this popup, you will select the payment method with which you would want to make this payment.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F60c3259d-df23-4c28-9924-92ce5884f745_1008x620.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F60c3259d-df23-4c28-9924-92ce5884f745_1008x620.png)

From the image illustration above, you will realize that there are a number of options to select from to complete this Remita payment, By default the card payment is selected. In upcoming articles, other payment methods will be explained and discussed.

**To complete using the card method:**

-   Enter card details and click the pay button

-   An OTP will be sent to the assigned contact tied to that card

-   Input the OTP in the given box and submit.

-   Congratulations, that went successful.
